---
title: SNMP Error Codes
description: "Error-status codes carried in SNMP PDUs, indicating the outcome of Get, GetNext, Set, and Inform operations. SNMPv1 codes (0–5) are defined in RFC 1157; SNMPv2c and SNMPv3 extended the set to codes 0–18 in RFC 3416. Also includes the generic trap types defined in RFC 1157 Section 4.1.6."
references:
  - https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc1157
  - https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc3416
