---
name: "Emulator trap"
numeric: 7
description: "macOS/BSD-specific signal generated by an emulator trap instruction. Signal 7 on macOS/BSD; not defined on Linux (where signal 7 is SIGBUS). Rarely triggered in modern applications; the default action is to terminate the process with a core dump."
references:
  - https://developer.apple.com/library/archive/documentation/System/Conceptual/ManPages_iPhoneOS/man2/signal.2.html
  - https://man.freebsd.org/cgi/man.cgi?signal(3)
