---
name: "Insufficient Storage"
description: "Defined by WebDAV (RFC 4918) to indicate that the method could not be performed because the server cannot store the representation needed to complete the request. Commonly returned by PUT or COPY operations when the target collection or server volume lacks sufficient space."
references:
  - https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc4918#section-11.5
---
