---
name: "Already Reported"
description: "Defined by RFC 5842 (DAV Binding Extensions) for use inside a 207 Multi-Status response to indicate that the binding's members have already been enumerated in a prior part of the same response and are not repeated. Prevents infinite loops when a resource is accessible via multiple DAV bindings."
references:
