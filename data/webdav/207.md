---
name: "Multi-Status"
description: "Defined by WebDAV (RFC 4918) to convey the status of multiple independent operations in a single response. The response body contains an XML document with individual `<D:status>` elements per resource, used by methods such as PROPFIND, PROPPATCH, COPY, MOVE, and LOCK when acting on multiple resources."
references:
  - https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc4918#section-11.1
