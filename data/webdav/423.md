---
name: "Locked"
description: "Defined by WebDAV (RFC 4918) to indicate that the source or destination resource of a method is locked, and the request did not supply a valid lock token. Returned by methods such as PUT, DELETE, MOVE, COPY, and PROPPATCH when the target resource has an active write lock."
references:
  - https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc4918#section-11.3
---
